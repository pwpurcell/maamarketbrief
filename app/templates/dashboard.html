Net flow positive = injection, negative = withdrawal. Days of cover = (inventory − cushion gas) ÷ max withdrawal rate at operator nameplate peak — the planning-relevant figure for stress events, not current flow. Cushion gas is the minimum physically immobile inventory below which withdrawal stops; Iona's ~6 PJ floor is per the 2023 GSOO (so working gas at full = 18.5 PJ, not 24.5). Other facilities aren't published in the same detail; their cushion is treated as 0 (working = full inventory) until firmer data arrives.
